  • Price: Enter the price of the item you wish to purchase.

  • Cash Drawer: The cash drawer contains various denominations, including pennies, nickels, dimes, quarters, ones, fives, tens, twenties, and hundreds.
  • Purchase Button: Click the "Purchase" button to calculate the change due.

Based on the cash provided and the cash in the drawer, the app will display one of the following statuses:

  • Status: INSUFFICIENT_FUNDS - The cash in the drawer is not enough to provide the correct change, or it's not possible to give exact change.
  • Status: CLOSED - The cash in the drawer is exactly equal to the change due.
  • Status: OPEN - The cash in the drawer is more than the change due, and exact change can be given. The change will be listed in descending order of denominations.

Try entering different prices and cash amounts to see how the app responds.
